"Charity Foundation: 1. Donation Management: Individuals can donate to our foundation for various causes (e.g., flood relief, education for underprivileged students, food distribution).

2. Event Management: We organize and manage events like flood relief drives, student support programs, and food donations.
Events are tailored to address urgent community needs and maximize impact.

Loans are intended to help individuals improve their economic conditions and gain financial stability."
